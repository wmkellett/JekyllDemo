Summary Dashboard

The Summary Dashboard provides general information about all the transfers and checkpoints that have occurred within the last 30 to 120 minutes in the current VersaLex pool. You control the time period through the top bar.

Unlike other dashboards within the Cleo Dashboards™ software, the Summary Dashboard is configured to refresh automatically every 60 seconds. All other dashboards are configured for manual refresh.
