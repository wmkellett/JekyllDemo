Changing Transfer Dashboard chart filter, category, and series

The Go to by Value options are similar to the Go To options; the difference is that the Go to by Value options will filter the data in addition to changing the category/series. Using the original chart, to reclassify the series by, for instance, Status rather than General Status, and to filter by Failed transfers, use the following steps:

  1. Right-click on the Failed legend label along the side of the chart. 
  2. Expand the Go to by Value submenu by hovering over the label.
  3. Click on the submenu item Status to render the chart with the new parameters. 

These steps demonstrate the Go to by Value options to change and filter the series; the steps are the same for changing and filtering the category.
