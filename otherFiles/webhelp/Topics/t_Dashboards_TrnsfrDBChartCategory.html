Changing Transfer Dashboard chart category and series

To change the chart category:

  1. Right-click the Trading Partner label along the bottom of the x-axis to display a menu. 
  2. Hover over Go To to display the submenu. 
  3. Choose from the options for new categorization: General Status, Host\Mailbox, or Status. The chart is redrawn with the new x-axis category.

To change the chart series:

  1. Right-click any of the legend labels along the side of the chart. 
  2. Hover over Go To to display the submenu. 
  3. Choose from the options for new series classification: General Status, Host\Mailbox, and Status. The chart is redrawn with the new series.
