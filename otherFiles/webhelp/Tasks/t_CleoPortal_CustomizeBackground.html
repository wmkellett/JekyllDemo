Customizing Cleo Portal login page graphics

You can customize the look of your Cleo Portal login page by replacing the graphics files displayed by the product.

Navigate to and replace the following files:
  • <installDir>/webserver/mftportal/images/layout/background.png - login screen background image.
  • <installDir>/webserver/mftportal/images/layout/logo.png - login screen logo.
  • <installDir>/webserver/mftportal/images/layout/logo-header.png - header logo.
Note: If you re-run the installer, the original graphics files are reinstalled with the product and you must make this customization again.
